Stay Safe Online: Simple Checklist (For College Students)
Easy and Practical tips:
1. Use a strong password (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and don’t reuse it on multiple apps.
2. Turn on 2-step verification (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps.
3. Do not click on unknown links sent via WhatsApp, SMS, or email.
4. Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
5. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into bank or personal accounts.
6. Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
7. Always check carefully before approving any payment request (Pay vs Collect).
8. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone.
UPI / Payment Safety:
 Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. PIN is only needed when sending money.
 Always check if it is a “Pay” or “Request” before approving on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
 Do not scan unknown QR codes or trust random payment requests.
 Never share your OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone.
 Report Fraud:
 Report online at National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)
 Call 1930 immediately
