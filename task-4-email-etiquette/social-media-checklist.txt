Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist 

 

Do’s (What You Should Do) 

Think before posting anything online  

Keep your profile professional and respectful  

Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings  

Share useful and positive content  

Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news)  

Shape 

 Don’ts (What You Should Avoid) 

Don’t share personal information (address, phone number, OTP)  

Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content  

Don’t accept requests from unknown people  

Don’t waste too much time scrolling (avoid addiction)  

Don’t engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
